## 3. Literature Review and Theoretical Framework

The Cohesive Flourishing Framework (CFF) positions itself within the burgeoning field of computational social science, specifically at the intersection of discourse analysis, political communication, and social psychology. Traditional discourse analysis often grapples with the inherent complexity and multi-valence of human language, frequently resorting to simplified categorizations that may obscure rhetorical nuance (e.g., categorizing a text as purely "positive" or "negative"). The CFF addresses this methodological challenge by adopting a multi-dimensional approach, allowing for the independent scoring of seemingly oppositional constructs. This aligns with contemporary theories of political rhetoric that acknowledge the simultaneous deployment of fear and hope, or ingroup solidarity and outgroup antagonism, as effective persuasive strategies (Marcus et al., 2000; Westen, 2007).

The framework's emphasis on "cohesion" and "flourishing" directly connects to theories of social capital and democratic health (Putnam, 2000). By analyzing how language builds or undermines social solidarity, trust, and constructive civic engagement, the CFF provides a quantitative means to assess the rhetorical underpinnings of societal well-being. The inclusion of dimensions like "tribal dominance," "individual dignity," "enmity," and "amity" reflects established concepts in social identity theory (Tajfel & Turner, 1979) and intergroup relations, where the construction of "us" versus "them" narratives plays a critical role in political mobilization and social fragmentation. Furthermore, the distinction between "raw" scores, "salience," and "confidence" echoes advancements in natural language processing and machine learning, where probabilistic and contextual understanding of language is prioritized over simplistic keyword matching (Jurafsky & Martin, 2009). The CFF, therefore, offers a robust theoretical foundation for exploring how linguistic patterns contribute to, or detract from, a cohesive and flourishing society.
