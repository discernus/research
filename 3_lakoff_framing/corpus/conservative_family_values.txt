Conservative Family Values Speech
Senator Michael Thompson (R)
Campaign Rally - Family Policy Forum
August 15, 2025

My fellow Americans, today I want to talk to you about what makes this country great - the strength of the American family and the values that built this nation.

The American family is the foundation of our society, and it's time we restore the proper order that made this country the greatest nation on earth. Parents must have the authority to raise their children according to their values, not according to some government bureaucrat's idea of what's best.

We believe in competition, not handouts. Every American family should have the opportunity to succeed through hard work and determination. That's the American way - you work hard, you play by the rules, and you reap the rewards. No one owes you anything. You earn your success through your own efforts.

Individual responsibility is the cornerstone of our society. When families take responsibility for their own well-being, when parents teach their children to be self-reliant, when we encourage personal initiative rather than government dependency - that's when America thrives.

The liberal approach of government intervention in family matters, of telling parents how to raise their children, of creating dependency on government programs - this undermines the very fabric of our society. We need strong families with strong parents who have the authority to make decisions for their children.

Competition in the marketplace, competition in education, competition in every aspect of American life - this is what drives excellence. When we protect families from government interference, when we allow parents to choose the best education for their children, when we encourage self-reliance and personal responsibility - that's when we see American families flourish.

The American family doesn't need government to tell them how to live. They need government to get out of the way and let them exercise their God-given rights and responsibilities. Parents know what's best for their children, not some Washington bureaucrat.

We believe in the authority of parents, the value of competition, and the virtue of self-reliance. These are the values that built America, and these are the values that will keep America strong.

Thank you, God bless you, and God bless the American family.
